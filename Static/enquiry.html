Introduction

The AIBD conference consists of keynote talks and tutorial talks by world-leading scientists. The keynote speakers include

·         Prof. Qiang Shen, Professor and Pro Vice-Chancellor of Aberystwyth University, United Kingdom,

·         Prof. Jiannong Cao, Dean of the Graduate School and Chair Professor of Distributed and Mobile Computing of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong SAR,

·         Prof. Gwo-Jen Hwang, Chair Professor of the Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taiwan,

·         Prof. Amir Hussain, Professor of Edinburgh Napier University, United Kingdom,

·         Prof. Yanchun Zhang, Professor of Victoria University, Australia, and

·         Prof. Guandong Xu, Professor of University of Technology Sydney, Australia.

 

The tutorial speakers include

·         Prof. Erik Cambria, Associate Professor of Nanyang Technological University, Singapore,

·         Prof. Yi Cai, Professor and Dean of South China University of Technology, China,

·         Prof. Hui-Chun Chu, Distinguished Professor of Soochow University, Taiwan,
